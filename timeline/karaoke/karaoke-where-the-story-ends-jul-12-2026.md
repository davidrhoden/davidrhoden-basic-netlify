I saw the video while in a YouTube rabbit hole. It's so appealing; the singer is so petite and pretty, but they didn't doll her up and make her do anything like spin around in a field in slow mottion; she just pretends to sing the song in a dream rehearsal space (looks to be as big as a church) with her band mates, who also get a little screen time. She has her hair up and she's wearing, like, a sweater and jeans. Too bad they didn't have any other songs (that I 'm aware of).

I'm pretty sure I transcribed the lyric wrong but I just wrote down what I heard. I didn't think about it too much. I was thinkng about what a terrible year this has been.
Remember when the guy tried to rush the hotel and shoot the President, but he didn't even get close? I thought that was a year ago. It was late April. The economy has gotten even worse since then.
