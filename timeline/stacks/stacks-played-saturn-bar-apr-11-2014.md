Thanks to "dylanjames67" (I believe his real name is Dylan James Stansbury) for filming this. I really had a wild time watching this. For one thing I have the opposite of a bad body image; in my mind I'm much thinner and have more hair. Maybe I don't look so bad to other people, I don't know. I also realize how much my brother and I look alike. I liked being able to see myself playing guitar--I've never considered myself to be "good at" playing, I guess I feel like an impostor, like a late starter. (I think I was over thirty when I first got on a stage with a guitar in my hands.)

The bit where I (gently) push the guy off the stage is funny. He was an enthusiastic fan but also a distracting one. I never saw the pictures he took, but thanks a lot ofr coming out.
