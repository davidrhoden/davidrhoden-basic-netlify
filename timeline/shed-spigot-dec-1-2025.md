I installed a spigot in my backyard shed. It's something I'd been contemplating for years I guess. We had no way to run a hose here in the backyard, and now we do. The piping is PEX, and the spigot itself was a "Shark Bite" (connects with no tools), so it was easy. I'm very happy with it.
