 I dreamed my friend Melanie and I worked together. I think we were architects. The client was a big church. Their represntatives came to our office and took over our desks. They also each (there were two) brought an "AI" assistant, but the assistants were life-like androids, indistinguishable from people, except their feelings were more easily hurt, and they had to be in physical contact with the person they were assisting at all times. We had to meet with them in a car. I think it was just too hard to get the assistants set up anywhere else. They were still unhelpful, they just whined about the very concept of changing the church buildings.

 Melanie and I discussed just getting a big table to put our laptops on instead of asking for our desks back. I felt like this was a satisfying, passive-aggressive thing to do.

Also, the church had three bus drivers who were just sitting around, and one of them tried to pick a fight with me, or prove I wouldn't fight him. He did the shoulder thing guys sometimes to to show dominance when passing, but he initiated the shoulder bump then fell to the floor (the aisle of the bus) as if I had really shoved him hard, and he cried out in a silly voice that I think was suppopsed to be an impression of me.
