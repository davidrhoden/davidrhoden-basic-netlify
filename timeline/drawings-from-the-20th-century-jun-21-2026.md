You may have never guessed this, but I have several boxes full of silly little drawings I did, on Post-It notes and index cards, on the backs of receipts and bank statements, on random sheets of paper from the garbage. I'd like to be shed of them, so I can put something else in the boxes, such as money, but I can't bear the though of dumping them all in the trash at once, like what will happen when I die. So I made a little zine of a few special ones that I found while sorting through the boxes of detritus. I scanned them and _then_ I threw them away.

There's no big meaning behind it. It's just a little glimpse into how my mind works (or doesn't).
