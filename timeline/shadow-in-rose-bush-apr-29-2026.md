![My cat Shadow sitting in a rose bush](/static/img/shadow/shadow-in-rosebush-apr-29-2026.jpeg)

I noticed both cats, Shadow and Buddy, were hanging out by the rose bush. I wondered why. So I went over to check, and noticed there was a bird in the cypress tree that I couldn't see, chattering and squawking.

Buddy was sitting regally on the fence, but where was Shadow? Oh, there she was, in the rosebush. And not only that, she was sitting in a bird's nest, likely the nest of the bird squawking angrily in the tree.

![The bird's nest she was sitting in, with eggs (blue eggs with goldish brown spots)](/static/img/shadow/nest-of-eggs-in-rosebush-apr-29-2026.jpeg)
My mom thought these were blue jay eggs, which makes sense, I see a lot of them.

Here's Prince Buddy, looking princely.

![Buddy the regal cat sitting on the fence.](/static/img/buddy/buddy-sitting-on-fence-apr-29-2026.jpeg)

-----

Later that day I took a walk on the levee. While I was up there, I found the strangest beehive. It was in a mobile crane. A little online research tells me this part might be called the "boom head".

![Crane with beehive in the boom head](/static/img/timeline/crane-with-beehive-apr-29-2026.jpeg)
