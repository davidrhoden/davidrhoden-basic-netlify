Ariel asked me if I watched The Smurfs, or was that too late for me. I texted back "way too late. We watched The Pink Panther and The Ant and The Aardvark." I was trying to sound dated. But I duckduckgo-ed The Ant and the Aardvark, and found [a season of it on archive.org](https://archive.org/details/the-ant-and-the-aardvark).

I'd forgotten how beautifully drawn this show was. I'd like to give credit, but I don't know who drew what. So here are some screenshots from the credits. Also, look at that lineup of musicians!
