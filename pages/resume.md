Even with AI, the prompter needs a solid background in how websites and software actually work to create a workable plan for AI to implement. The output from LLMs is frankly amazing, and it comes together so fast, but it still needs human improvement. Maybe the computers will eliminate the need for humans altogether soon, but it hasn't happened yet.
